# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** February 3, 2026

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help us provide and improve our services by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our site.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

| Category | Purpose | Examples |
|----------|---------|----------|
| **Essential** | Website functionality | Session management, security |
| **Preferences** | Remember your settings | Language, display preferences |
| **Analytics** | Understand site usage | Google Analytics, visitor counts |
| **Marketing** | Measure ad effectiveness** | Conversion tracking |

**Marketing cookies are only used with your explicit consent.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 First-Party Cookies
Set by Robotica Weekly directly:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintain login session | Session |
| `cookie_consent` | Store cookie preferences | 1 year |
| `theme_preference` | Dark/light mode | 1 year |

### 3.2 Third-Party Cookies
Set by our service providers:

| Provider | Cookie | Purpose |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Google Analytics | `_ga`, `_gid` | Website analytics |
| SendGrid | `sg_cookies` | Email delivery tracking |

## 4. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner allowing you to:

- **Accept All** - Allow all cookie types
- **Essential Only** - Only required cookies
- **Customize** - Choose specific categories

You can change your preferences anytime by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer.

## 5. Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

### Opt-Out Options
- **Google Analytics:** https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- **Network Advertising Initiative:** http://optout.networkadvertising.org

## 6. Cookie Duration

- **Session cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Remain for a set period (1 month to 2 years)
- **Analytics cookies:** Typically 24 months maximum

## 7. Data Collected by Cookies

Cookies may collect:
- IP address (anonymized for analytics)
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website
- Download errors

Updates will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.
