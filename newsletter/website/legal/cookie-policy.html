This Cookie Policy explains how Robotica Weekly uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide analytics data.

Some cookies are placed by third-party services we use:

  • Google Analytics — Website traffic analysis

4. How to Manage Cookies

4.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our site, a banner asks for your consent to non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

4.2 Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

4.3 Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of specific third-party tracking:

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out
  • Facebook Ad Preferences

5. Cookie Consent Withdrawal

You can change your cookie preferences anytime by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer.

6. What Happens If You Decline Cookies

If you reject non-essential cookies:

  • The website will still function
  • You can still subscribe and read content
  • Analytics and personalization will be limited
  • Some features may not work optimally

7. Data Collected by Cookies

Cookies may collect:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
