- **Portability** — Receive your data in a machine-readable format

## Cookie Policy

### What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites.

### Types We Use

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_session` | Maintain login session | Session |
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics (throttle) | 1 minute |

### Managing Cookies
- **Browser settings** — Control cookies in your browser preferences
- **Opt-out** — Disable Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- **Email tracking** — Our emails include tracking pixels; disable images to prevent tracking

### Consent
By using our website, you consent to essential cookies. Analytics cookies require explicit consent (shown via banner).
