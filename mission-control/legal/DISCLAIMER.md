## Risk Factors

### Robotics & AI Investing Risks
Investing in robotics and AI companies involves **SUBSTANTIAL RISK**, including:

#### Technology Risk
- Products may fail to commercialize
- Technology may become obsolete
- R&D may not yield viable products
- Competition from larger tech companies

#### Market Risk
- High volatility in tech stocks
- Market sentiment changes rapidly
- Interest rate impacts on growth stocks
- Economic downturns affect capital availability

#### Regulatory Risk
- AI regulation is evolving (EU AI Act)
- Export controls on technology
- Data privacy requirements
- Autonomous systems liability laws

#### Company-Specific Risk
- Startups may fail entirely
- Key person dependency
- Intellectual property disputes
- Supply chain disruptions
- Capital raising challenges

#### Liquidity Risk
- Small-cap stocks have limited liquidity
- Private companies are illiquid
- Lock-up periods for IPOs
- Secondary market limitations

### Past Performance
**PAST PERFORMANCE DOES NOT GUARANTEE FUTURE RESULTS.**

- Stocks that performed well may decline

- Our past analysis may not predict future outcomes
