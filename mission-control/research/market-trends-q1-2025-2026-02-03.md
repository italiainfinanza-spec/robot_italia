### Critical Development: Section 232 Investigation into Robotics

**September 24, 2025:** The U.S. Commerce Department initiated a **Section 232 investigation** into robotics and industrial machinery under the Trade Expansion Act of 1962. This is the same legal authority used for steel and aluminum tariffs.

| Investigation Detail | Status |
|---------------------|--------|
| **Initiated** | September 24, 2025 |
| **Scope** | Robotics and industrial machinery |
| **Legal Authority** | Section 232 (national security) |
| **Potential Outcome** | Tariffs up to 25% on imports |
| **Affected Countries** | Likely all major exporters (China, EU, Japan, S. Korea) |
