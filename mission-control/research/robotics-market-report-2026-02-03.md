## Executive Summary

The robotics market is experiencing unprecedented growth, driven by breakthroughs in AI integration, the emergence of "Physical AI," and massive capital inflows. Global robotics funding exceeded $10.3 billion in 2025, with the humanoid robotics market alone projected to reach $15.26 billion by 2033 (CAGR 39.2%).

**Key Finding:** The convergence of AI and robotics is creating a new category—Physical AI—that enables robots to understand the real world, reason, and plan actions autonomously.
