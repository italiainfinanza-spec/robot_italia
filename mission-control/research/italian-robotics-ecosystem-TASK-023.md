## 3. Tax Incentives & Government Support

### 3.1 Industria 4.0 / Industry 5.0 Tax Credits

Italy's **Industria 4.0** (now evolving into **Industry 5.0**) is one of Europe's most attractive fiscal frameworks for robotics and automation investment.

| Incentive | Rate | Description | Valid Through |
|-----------|------|-------------|---------------|
| **R&D Tax Credit** | 50% | Credit on incremental R&D expenses | Extended |
| **Hyper-Depreciation** | 150% | Increased depreciation for Industry 4.0 equipment | 2025 (reviewed) |
| **Super-Depreciation** | 140% | Increased depreciation for new machinery | 2025 (reviewed) |
| **Technological Innovation Credit** | 5% | Credit for new tangible/intangible assets | Through 2025 |

### Bull Case
1. **Institutional Backing:** CDP Venture Capital (state-backed) is actively deploying capital
2. **Research Excellence:** IIT is globally recognized for robotics research
3. **Tax Efficiency:** Among Europe's most favorable R&D tax regimes
4. **Landmark Validation:** â‚¬70M Generative Bionics round proves large-ticket viability
5. **Corporate Interest:** Tether, AMD Ventures, Eni Next all actively investing

### Risk Factors
