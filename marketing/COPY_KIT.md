## üìã Usage Guidelines

### Tone of Voice
- **Confident but not arrogant**
- **Expert but accessible**
- **Fast-paced but thorough**
- **Professional but human**

### Forbidden Words
- "Game-changing" (unless quoting)
- "Revolutionary" (overused)
- "AI" without context (too vague)
- "Guaranteed" (compliance risk)
- "Get rich quick" (compliance risk)

### Required Disclaimers
- All investment content must include: *"Not financial advice."*
- Performance claims must include: *"Past performance doesn't indicate future results."*
- Subscription pages must link to full Terms of Service

### A/B Testing Priority
1. Headlines (highest impact)
2. CTA button text
3. Social proof placement
4. Pricing display (monthly vs. annual)
5. Email subject lines
