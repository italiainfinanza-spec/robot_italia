## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide
- **Email address** (required for newsletter subscription)
- **Name** (optional, for personalization)
- **Subscription preferences** (Free or Premium tier)
- **Payment information** (processed securely by our payment provider, never stored by us)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, time spent, click patterns

### 2.3 Legal Basis for Processing
- **Consent:** Newsletter subscriptions (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))
- **Contract:** Premium subscription fulfillment (GDPR Article 6(1)(b))
- **Legitimate interests:** Website analytics and security (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

1. **Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate information
3. **Erasure** ("Right to be Forgotten") — Request deletion
4. **Restriction** — Limit how we process your data
5. **Data Portability** — Receive data in a portable format
6. **Objection** — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
7. **Withdraw Consent** — Unsubscribe anytime

**To exercise your rights:**  
Email: privacy@roboticaweekly.com  
Response time: Within 30 days
