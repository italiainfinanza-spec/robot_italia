## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

### 7.1 Right to Access (Art. 15)
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Art. 17)
You can request deletion of your personal data when:
- The data is no longer necessary
- You withdraw consent
- You object to processing and we have no overriding legitimate grounds

### 7.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18)
You can request limitation of processing in specific circumstances.

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
You can request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

### 7.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)
You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7)
You can withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

### 7.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint (Art. 77)
You have the right to complain to a supervisory authority:
