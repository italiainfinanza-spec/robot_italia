## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help us:
- Remember your preferences
- Understand how you use our site
- Improve your experience
- Provide relevant content

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)
These cookies are necessary for the website to function. You cannot disable them.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Security protection | Session |
| `cookie_consent` | Stores your cookie preferences | 1 year |

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)
Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguish users | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Distinguish users | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics | Throttle request rate | 1 minute |

### 2.3 Preference Cookies (Optional)
Remember your settings and choices.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `theme_preference` | Dark/light mode setting | 1 year |
| `newsletter_format` | Preferred email format | 1 year |

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies (Optional)
Used to deliver relevant advertisements (we use minimal marketing cookies).

## 4. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie banner. You can:

- **Accept All:** Allow all cookie types
- **Essential Only:** Only required cookies
- **Customize:** Select which categories to allow

**To change your preferences:**
1. Click the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer
2. Adjust your choices
3. Save changes

---

## 5. How to Manage Cookies in Your Browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

### Chrome
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
2. Choose your preferred settings

### Firefox
1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
2. Select your cookie preferences

### Safari
1. Preferences → Privacy
2. Manage cookie settings

### Edge
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
2. Adjust cookie controls

**Note:** Blocking cookies may affect website functionality.
