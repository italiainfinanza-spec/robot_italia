## Mobile-First Design Rules

### Scannability
1. Use emojis as visual anchors
2. Bold key numbers/facts
3. Short paragraphs (2-3 lines)
4. White space between sections
5. Clear hierarchy (headers, subheaders)

### Thumb-Friendly
1. Large tap targets for links
2. CTA buttons at least 44px tall
3. Single column layout
4. No horizontal scrolling

### Load Time
1. Minimal images (or lazy load)
2. Inline CSS (no external stylesheets)
3. Under 100KB total size

---

## Section Priority (If Space Limited)

**Must Have:**
1. Header/Branding
2. Opening hook
3. Top 3 stories
4. One CTA
5. Footer

**Nice to Have:**
- Trend of the day
- Quick bites
- Events
- Social share

**Premium Only:**
- Deep dive
- Full market data
- Entry points
- ETF analysis

## Length Guidelines

| Section | Daily Premium | Weekly Free |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Total length | 800-1200 words | 400-600 words |
| Subject line | 40-50 chars | 40-50 chars |
| Opening | 50-75 words | 40-60 words |
| Per story | 75-100 words | 100-150 words |
| Deep dive | 200-300 words | N/A |
| Reading time | 5-7 minutes | 3-4 minutes |

## Visual Hierarchy

**H1:** Logo/Title (28px)
**H2:** Section headers (18px, uppercase)
**H3:** Story headlines (16px, bold)
**Body:** 14-15px, line-height 1.6
**Caption/Meta:** 12px, gray

---

## Preheader Text Optimization

The preheader (preview text) appears after the subject line in the inbox:
- **Length:** 40-100 characters
- **Purpose:** Complements (don't repeat) the subject line
- **Hidden in body:** Use CSS to hide it from email body
- **Make it count:** Second chance to get the open

**Examples:**
- Subject: "Vention: $110M per conquistare l'America"
- Preheader: "Ecco perchÃ© questo round di funding cambia tutto..."

## Legal Requirements (GDPR/Italian Law)

### Required Footer Elements:
1. **Sender Physical Address** - Company registered address
2. **Clear Unsubscribe Link** - One-click preferred, must work within 48h
3. **Privacy Policy Link** - Link to full privacy policy
4. **"Why You're Receiving This"** - Brief explanation ("You subscribed to Robotica Weekly")
5. **Company VAT Number** - P.IVA (if applicable)
6. **Data Controller Info** - Company name and contact

### GDPR Compliance Checklist:
- [ ] Consent obtained lawfully (subscribers opted in)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link works and processes within 48 hours
- [ ] Data retention policy stated
- [ ] No purchased lists (EU prohibits this)
- [ ] Secure data storage
